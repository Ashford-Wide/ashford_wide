## Thinking of running a stall at an Ashford Wide event?

If you're considering holding a stall at one of our events there are specific pieces of information we'll need from you before the event.

### All stallholders

- Public liability insurance certificate

### Selling food and/or drink

- Food Standards Agency rating (for example: [Costa Coffee's](https://ratings.food.gov.uk/business/277624/costa-coffee-church-road))
- Gas safety inspection certificates
- Electrical safety inspection certificates

### Why do we need this?

Before running an event we have to share information with the local authority who check that all safety standards are met.
