One of the barriers that inhibits businesses growing and developing that is often nationally quoted, relates to the difficulties experienced dealing with various departments in the Local Council that businesses need to work with.

The intention of this seminar is to de-mystify the process and provide answers to questions that you need to know. We want to work with you, understand you and help you grow and flourish.

On 21st May 6pm -8pm there will be a Business Needs Seminar held in the Council Chamber at Spelthorne Council. This will consist of short and informative updates including any relevant latest legislation of about 15 minutes each by Spelthorne Council staff who are all experts in their own field.

This will mean that each segment of the presentations short, snappy and easier to absorb. The following areas will be covered: 

* Procurement of goods and services by the Council – how to do it
* Business Rates
* Planning
* The Environment (Green issues)
* Environmental Health

There will be also an opportunity for a question and answer session.

If you would like to register for this event, please contact Sam Ridley at [spelthornebusinessforum@spelthorne.gov.uk](mailto:spelthornebusinessforum@spelthorne.gov.uk)
