With their meeting with South Western Rail in sight at the end of this month, they are still accruing evidence to prove support and intent.

The petition has reached just under 200 short of 12,000 signatures and it would be advantageous to reach that figure.

Please share the link below with family, friends, work colleagues and neighbours. It is not necessary that you live in Spelthorne to add your name, as we believe more people from nearby towns, more visitors and workers from outside the area, would travel by our train lines rather than use motor vehicles.

They would also like to hear from you with personal descriptions of how you travel outside of our borough to stations with Oyster use to save money and for a more convenient link with the London Underground etc.

If you haven’t already given them your details, please include your home town and you will be added to their list of those already received to also use at the meeting. 

[https://www.change.org/p/spelthornezone6](https://www.change.org/p/spelthornezone6)
