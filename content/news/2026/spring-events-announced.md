We're thrilled to announce our spring and summer events programme for 2026.

This year's programme is our most ambitious yet, with a packed calendar of events designed to bring the community together, support local businesses and showcase everything that makes Ashford such a special place.

## Upcoming Highlights

**Ashford Spring Festival — 18 April 2026**
Our popular Spring Festival returns to County Square with live music, local food producers and family activities. Free entry for all.

**Summer Market — 11 July 2026**
The summer market is back on Ashford High Street, celebrating local produce, artisan goods and community spirit.
