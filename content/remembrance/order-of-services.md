## Remembrance Sunday Order of Service

Each year on Remembrance Sunday, Ashford comes together to pay tribute to those who gave their lives in the service of their country.

The service is held at the War Memorial in Ashford town centre and is led by local clergy, attended by representatives of the armed forces, local government, community organisations and members of the public.

## Order of Service

1. Gathering and procession to the War Memorial
2. Welcome and opening prayer
3. Act of Remembrance
4. The Last Post
5. Two minutes' silence
6. Reveille
7. Laying of wreaths
8. Prayers
9. National Anthem
10. March past

## Getting There

The War Memorial is located in the town centre. Please arrive by 10:45am to ensure the service begins promptly at 11:00am.
