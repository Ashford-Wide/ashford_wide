New for 2026 Ashford Wide are opening up Event Sponsorships giving even more options for your business to support Ashford and get your business noticed in the community. 

> [!NOTE]
> [View our current sponsorship opportunities](/current-sponsorship-opportunities)

It's a great way to show your commitment to Ashford, connect with local people, and gain valuable visibility at the town's biggest events, all whilst putting your brand in front of thousands of residents throughout the year.

## Benefits of Sponsorship

- Part or exclusive event sponsorship opportunities to suit your business and budget.
- Gain exposure across event signage, social media, printed materials and more, with customisable options.
- Have your brand associated with community and family events, great for brand image and recognition.
- If you're not ready to commit to Ashford Wide Business Membership, it's a great way to make your brand visible with Ashford's biggest community group.
- Multi-event options throughout the year to keep your business recognisable and current.

## What you can expect from an Ashford Wide Sponsorship

Sponsorship benefits vary depending on the package and event, but may include:

- Recognition as an official event sponsor.
- Your logo displayed on event signage and promotional materials.
- Promotion across Ashford Wide's social media channels before, during and after the event.
- Space to display your own promotional banner at the event.
- A complimentary stall pitch to showcase your products or services (where applicable).
- Bespoke sponsorship opportunities for selected events.
