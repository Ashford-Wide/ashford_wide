Join us for Ashford Rotary Club's Classic Vehicle Rally on Sunday 3rd September 2023.

There'll be:

- Auto jumble
- Trade stands
- Refreshments including a licenced bar
- And lots more!

If you're interested in registering your vehicle please visit [Ashford Rotary's website](https://rotary-ribi.org/clubs/homepage.php?ClubID=860).

This event is in support of Queen Elizabth's Foundation for Disabled People, more details on their work can be found on [their website](https://www.qef.org.uk/)
