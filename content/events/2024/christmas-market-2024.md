Ashford Christmas Market returns on Wednesday 4 December 5–8pm in Church Road, Ashford. We are joined by Staines and Lammas brass band for our carol singing, and Jimmy Kent will be leading some old-time Christmassy swing tunes for you all to listen too with your mulled wine.

Santa will be visiting and the grotto is free for all to visit in Ashford Library. We will also have some rides for the little ones, face painting and our Christmas bar, Christmas food and drink and Christmas music galore. That’s before you get to the all shopping stalls for gifts, decorations and treats.
 
We hope you can join us for what is always a fabulous evening, with lots to do for all the family.
