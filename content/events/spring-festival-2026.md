The Ashford Spring Festival returns to County Square, bringing the community together to celebrate the season with a packed programme of entertainment, food and activities.

## What's On

- Local food and drink producers
- Live music on the main stage throughout the day
- Children's craft activities and workshops
- Gardening and planting demonstrations
- Local business stalls and pop-ups

## Getting There

County Square is in the heart of Ashford town centre, a short walk from Ashford International station and all town centre car parks.

Entry is free for all.
