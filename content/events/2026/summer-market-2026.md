Our popular summer market returns to Ashford High Street, showcasing the best of local food, crafts and community spirit.

Whether you're looking for fresh produce, handmade gifts or simply a great day out, the Ashford Summer Market has something for everyone.

## Highlights

- Fresh local produce and artisan food stalls
- Handmade crafts and gifts from local makers
- Street food and refreshments
- Live entertainment
- Family-friendly activities

Entry is free and all are welcome.
