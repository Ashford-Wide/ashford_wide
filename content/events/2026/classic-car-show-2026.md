The Ashford Classic Car Show is back, hosted by Ashford Rotary, with hundreds of classic and old cars and motorbikes.

There will be:

- Auto Jumble
- Trade Stands & Refreshments
- Licenced Bar & Lots More

## Register your vehicle

To register a spot to show off your vehicle, please visit [Ashford Rotary's site](https://ashfordrotary.org.uk/home-pre-booked-registrations/). There is a minium donation of £10.
