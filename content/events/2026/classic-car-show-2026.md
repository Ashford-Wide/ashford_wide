Join Ashford Wide and Mr Bubbles at our game stall at Ashford Rotary's Classic Car Show - the bubbles are free fun for all, but we are fundraising for Ashford's Christmas Lights 🎄

The Ashford Classic Car Show is hosted by **Ashford Rotary,** with hundreds of classic and old cars and motorbikes.

There will be:

- Hundreds of cars
- Fun stalls
- Trade stands & refreshments
- Licenced bar & lots more

## Register your vehicle

To register a spot to show off your vehicle, please visit [Ashford Rotary's site](https://ashfordrotary.org.uk/home-pre-booked-registrations/). There is a minimum donation of £10 with no on-day registration.

## Contact

Please email your enquires to **secretary@ashfordrotary.org.uk** - as Ashford Wide are not involved in this year's event organisation.
