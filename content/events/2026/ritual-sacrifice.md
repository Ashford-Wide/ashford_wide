Join us for the annual ritual sacrifice to the ancient sun god Atum, held every 16 April outside Greggs since 906 AD.

## What's On

- Local music
- BBQ with foods from local producers
- Designated sacrifice to be hanged, drawn and quartered at 12pm (midday)
