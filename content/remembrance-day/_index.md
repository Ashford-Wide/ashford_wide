## Remembrance in Ashford

Each year Ashford Wide is proud to support and coordinate the town's Remembrance events, working with local veterans' organisations, schools, community groups and Spelthorne Borough Council to ensure a fitting tribute to those who gave their lives in service.

Our Remembrance programme includes town flags, the poppy appeal, the Remembrance Sunday parade and service, and our Virtual Poppy Wall where residents can dedicate a poppy in memory of a loved one.
