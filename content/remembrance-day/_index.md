## Remembrance in Ashford

Each year Ashford Wide is proud to support and coordinate the town's Remembrance events, working with local veterans' organisations and other community groups, to ensure a fitting tribute to those who gave their lives in service.

Our Remembrance programme includes town flags, the Remembrance Sunday parade and service,  placing sponsored poppies around town, and our Virtual Poppy Wall where residents can dedicate a poppy in memory of a loved one.
