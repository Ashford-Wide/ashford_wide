## Virtual Poppy Wall

Our Virtual Poppy Wall is a free online memorial where anyone can place a virtual poppy in memory of a loved one who served in the armed forces, or simply to pay their respects to all who gave their lives in service.

## Add Your Poppy

To add a poppy to the Virtual Poppy Wall, please [contact us](/contact) with:

- Your name
- The name of the person you'd like to remember
- A brief message or dedication (optional)

Your poppy and dedication will be added to the wall, which is displayed here on this page during the Remembrance period.

## Why We Do This

We believe it's important that every member of our community has the opportunity to pay their respects, wherever they are. The Virtual Poppy Wall allows people who may not be able to attend services in person, whether due to distance, health or other reasons, to still participate in this important act of remembrance.

To find out more about our Remembrance activities, visit our [Remembrance page](/remembrance) or [contact us](/contact).
