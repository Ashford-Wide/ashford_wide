A number of roads in Ashford town centre will be closed on Remembrance Sunday to allow the procession to take place safely.

## Closure times

Roads will be closed from **10.30am to 11.30am**. Please plan your journey accordingly and allow extra time if you are travelling through the town centre.

## Affected roads

The following roads will be partially closed:

- Church Road
- Dudley Road
- Clarendon Road
- Fordbridge Road
