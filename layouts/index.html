About Ashford Wide

Our vision is to make Ashford a thriving town centre the whole community is proud of.

Ashford Wide is a not-for-profit Business Improvement District (BID) working in partnership with local businesses, community groups and Ashford Borough Council to deliver projects and services that improve the town centre experience for everyone.

From events and promotions to safety initiatives and environmental improvements, we're here to make a real difference for businesses and visitors alike.
